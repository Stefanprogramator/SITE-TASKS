A culinary adventure

-Top chefs, Top food-

Seasonal sensations

Be enticed by the flavours of Europe aboard the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express. Set menus that celebrate exceptional artisanal cuisine are an inclusive part of your trip. Drinks and à la carte options are also available at an additional charge. Don’t forget to inform us of dietary requirements in advance for a culinary journey that’s tailored to you.


Celebration breakfast

ndulge in a special breakfast prepared by our Executive Chef. A tempting selection of fresh breads, smoked salmon, eggs, truffles and caviar make a magical morning feast. Available on all southbound journeys and can be booked on board.

Champagne bar

Sample the finest champagnes in our exclusive bar. Art deco interiors and Lalique glassware conjure the romance and glamour of the Roaring Twenties. Vintages include Taittinger, Laurent Perrier Rosé and Louis Roederer Cristal—perfectly chilled.
